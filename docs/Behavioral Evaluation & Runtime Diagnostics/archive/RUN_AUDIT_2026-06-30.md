### Voice — a Phase-2 regression (found + fixed mid-run)

   guard** — a goal routed through more than one failure path re-counted into
   `goals_failed` *and* re-inflicted the full emotional penalty (impasse +
   reward_negative) each time, quietly pumping the negative signals.
2. **#6 introspection skew (93% / 0%):** the causal-frontier generator is purely

   contribute. The pick-time fairness bias can't help when the candidate *pool* is

   This is a surface of the brain-in-a-vat problem (direction doc Part C/#4).

   drift — the reconciler self-heals ~3/epoch but the *source* remains. Per
   `NEXT_RUN_TESTS` this is no longer deferrable: it's the **GOAL_STORE_UNIFICATION**
   work, an architecture task, not a patch.

## Follow-up finding (live run): energy never breathes — PROPOSED recalibration

Observed on the restarted clean-voice life: his **energy sits ~96% almost constantly**
(`resource_deficit` ≈ 0.037), which is not how a mind that has been working for hours
should feel. Constant high energy carries no behavioral signal.

**Why it's pinned high** (`update_signal_state.py` resource_deficit block):
- The law pulls `resource_deficit` toward a low baseline (allostatic τ ≈ 0.15 →
  ~85% energy even at rest), and cost-prediction *ease* nudges (work cheaper than he
  predicted) drain it below that → ~96%.
- Accumulation is a flat **+0.002/cycle** — ~12× weaker than the 0.025/cycle recovery,
  so fatigue can never climb; it equilibrates near the low baseline.
- The exhaustion dynamics (accelerated recovery + allostatic-load forcing) only arm
  above `resource_deficit > 0.60`, which is unreachable, so `_allostatic_load` stays 0.

**`resource_deficit` is load-bearing** — ~20 consumers read it (action gate, speech
pipeline/gate, selection features, binding, working memory, consolidation). Making it
breathe will make those actually respond to fatigue (the point), so it must be moved
deliberately, not slammed.

**Proposed change (allostatic — the Phase-1B spirit): make the setpoint shift with
sustained work** instead of a flat trickle:
- Replace the flat +0.002 accumulation with one **scaled by the cycle's cognitive
  cost / activity** (heavy thinking tires; idle doesn't).
- Keep recovery toward τ, so rest pulls it back down.
- Target arc: **~70–75% energy rested → ~30–40% after a long grind → recovering with
  rest.** Energy that breathes with his activity rather than flatlining.

**Status: APPLIED 2026-06-30** (`update_signal_state.py`, resource_deficit block).
The flat `+0.002` accumulation was replaced with an **activity-scaled** term:
`resource_deficit += 0.0025 + 0.010 × activity`, where `activity ∈ [0,1]` comes from
this cycle's measured cognitive cost (`context["_cost_prediction"]["actual_ms"] / 800`),
falling back to `energy_mode` (rest 0.2 / reactive 0.5 / active 0.75), then 0.5.
Recovery toward the idle-deepening allostatic τ is unchanged, so rest still restores.

Simulated arc at τ=0.15 (confirmed): **idle ~71% energy → typical-active ~54% →
sustained-heavy ~36%**, recovering to ~68% after ~100 idle cycles. Energy now breathes
with his activity instead of pinning near 100%. 753 brain tests green, lint clean.
Live-watch item: confirm energy actually swings over a run and that no downstream
consumer (action gate, speech) over-throttles when he tires.
