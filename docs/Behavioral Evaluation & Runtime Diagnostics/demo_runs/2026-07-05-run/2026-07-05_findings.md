fix shape. F1–F3 are gate blockers; F4–F7 are environment; F8–F9 are hygiene.

## F1 🔴 compose_section is a template stamper on an unstoppable treadmill

**Evidence:** 197 KB manuscript = 664 paragraphs / 4 unique; 166 sections in
~20 h at daemon cadence; goal's step 3 still `pending` at death; ledger paid
~0 (156/166 deduped) yet the loop never stopped; `compose_section` EMA 0.48
(neutral) after ~160 zero-reward reps.

**Three stacked bugs:**
1. **No content source.** `compose_section` emits fixed boilerplate with the
   title spliced in. It never draws from memory, notes, ledger effects, or
   the causal graph — the material a synthesis is supposed to synthesize.
2. **Step never completes or fails.** The retried step ("Write one concrete
   thing learned…") neither marks done on success nor escalates after N
   attempts — `_step_attempts` sat empty. 146 retries is not an attempt count
   any policy sanctioned.
3. **Daemon lane is learning-blind.** Zero-reward outcomes in the executive
   daemon never post to `action_reward_ema`, so A4's multiplicative demotion
   can't see the run's biggest waste. This is Run 2's lane split (metering)
   reborn as a learning gap.

**Fix shape:** (a) feed `compose_section` from real stores (recent ledger
effects, learned notes, causal edges on the goal's topic) and fail-closed if
the pool is empty — *"nothing to synthesize"* is a legitimate step failure;
(b) attempt cap on daemon step retries with escalation to goal failure;
(c) post daemon-lane effect outcomes (novelty×significance) into the same EMA
the conscious lane learns from.

**Run 5 shows:** a manuscript whose sections cite sources, or an honest
`no_artifact_by_deadline`; `compose_section` EMA visibly moves; no step
retried >5× without status change.

## F2 🔴 Aspirations are fail-able and one died

**Evidence:** 40/140 failure rows target `aspiration-*` nodes round-robin
every ~30–60 min with reason `objective unmet after 2 attempts: ['?', '?']`;
`aspiration-output_producing` failed at 04:52:30Z and is absent from
goals_mem at death.

**Two bugs:** the failure machinery treats standing values as 2-attempt
tasks, and whatever renders the reason can't see the criteria (literal `?`).
The re-seed path masked this all run; death unmasked it.

**Fix shape:** tier guard — `tier: long_term`/aspiration nodes are exempt
from objective-based failure (they can only be *edited*, never failed); fix
the criteria rendering; add a boot-time invariant that all four aspirations
exist, restoring from CORE_BASELINES if not.

**Run 5 shows:** 0 failure rows with `goal_id` starting `aspiration-`; all
four aspirations present at death.

## F3 🔴 The run's only good writing was destroyed by its own memory hygiene

**Evidence:** 23 satiety learned notes (novelty up to 0.93, 1.1–2.0 KB) exist
as ledger rows; no queryable store contains their bodies at death.
long_memory (2,001 entries) holds external scrapings instead — including
2,000 chars of raw Twitter CSS. Pruner "summarized and merged" and C3.10
decay are the suspects.

**Fix shape:** learned notes and produce_and_check outputs are **artifacts,
not memories** — persist bodies to `effect_artifacts/` (content-addressed,
like the first 10 manuscript sections already are) so hygiene can't eat them;
exempt ledger-referenced content from pruning; add a minimum
signal-to-markup gate on external intake before it becomes a memory
(strip CSS/JS; reject <50% prose).

**Run 5 shows:** every `note_novel` ledger row's hash resolves to a readable
body at death; no long_memory entry contains stylesheet text.

## F5 🟠 Candidate-generator monopoly: 1,270 of 1,508 candidates, 1 completion

**Evidence:** aspiration_scoreboard — "Understand the world more deeply"
generated 1,270 candidates (84%), completed 1; `generate_intrinsic_goals` is
again the #1 conscious action (45/183 tail decisions); funnel is
1,508 generated → 224 attempted → 51 completed.

The ignition-layer diet (B1) worked — the monopoly relocated upstream to the
*generator*, which burns the #1 action slot producing candidates that die
unattempted.

**Fix shape:** the same habituation idea one layer up — per-aspiration
candidate quota keyed to recent *attempt* (not generation) rate; demote
`generate_intrinsic_goals` when the unattempted-candidate pool is deep
(pool-depth term in its score, or fold generation into the B4-style 50% cap).

**Run 5 shows:** generated:attempted ratio < 3:1; no aspiration >50% of
generated candidates.

## F6 🟡 90-second frontier churn regressed S2 (median duration 3,722 s → 85.5 s)

**Evidence:** 41/51 retained completions are "Understand X more deeply"
frontier children (14/14/13 of three titles), each a single research_topic
call. The understanding-goal churn S1 targeted is back via the frontier
generator.

**Fix shape:** frontier children should inherit a real definition-of-done
(the 3-step plan exists but steps 2–3 were skipped as "goal completed" every
time — completion fires after step 1). Require ≥2 plan steps or ≥1 milestone
before a frontier child may complete; or make the frontier step budget
scale with what the last step actually learned (novelty of its note).

**Run 5 shows:** median_seconds_to_complete back over 600 s; no title
completed >5× per life.

## F7 🟡 User speech leaks into the goal generator; outbound speech is one stuck sentence

**Evidence:** Ric's reply "What do you think?" → three
`Open question: What do you think?` funnel candidates (one with embedded
newline). Outbound: 388 utterances, all variants of "something present but
hard to name… Am I off on that?", 4 within 40 s.

**Fix shape:** inbound — user utterances are addressed *to* him; route to the
conversational/social path, never the intrinsic-question miner (provenance

habituation on express_state (same `eff = raw/(1+k·n_identical)` as B1, keyed
on utterance similarity) plus a minimum-interval floor.

**Run 5 shows:** no goal candidate whose title is a verbatim user utterance;
distinct-utterance ratio > 0.3.
