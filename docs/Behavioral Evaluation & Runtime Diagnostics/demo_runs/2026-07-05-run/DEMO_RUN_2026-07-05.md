## The headline artifact — and the headline bug

He wrote his first book-shaped thing:
`brain/data/tracked_work/turn-what-i-know-about-the-world-into-a-written-synthesis.md`
— **197 KB, 1,992 lines, 166 sections**, appended steadily for ~20 hours
(08:59Z Jul 5 → 04:52Z Jul 6, one section every ~7 minutes).

It is **one template stamped 166 times**: 664 body paragraphs, **4 unique**.
146 of the 166 section headings are the literal plan-step string *"Write one
concrete thing learned about Turn what I know about the world into a written
synthesis to working memory"*. `compose_section` emits fixed boilerplate with
the goal title spliced in; the step it serves never completes; the daemon
retries it forever.

The grounding layer **did its job**: 156/166 ledger rows deduped, novelty
crashed 1.0 → 0.0–0.06 by the tenth section, total significance across all
166 stamps ≈ 7.6. He earned nothing for it. He also never stopped. That is
the new signature: **not a reward leak — a zero-reward treadmill the selector
can't feel**, because the reward EMA never sees daemon-lane outcomes
(`compose_section` EMA still 0.48 at death).

## What else the run surfaced (full detail in findings doc)

1. **The failure machinery eats aspirations.** 40 of the 140 failures target
   the aspiration nodes themselves, round-robin, every ~30–60 min, all life,
   with the broken reason string `objective unmet after 2 attempts: ['?', '?']`.
   At 04:52:30Z — 100 seconds before death — it **failed
   `aspiration-output_producing` itself**, and the "Make things" value is gone
   from `goals_mem.json`.
2. **The learned notes evaporated.** The 23 satiety learned notes (the only
   genuinely novel prose of the run, novelty up to 0.93) exist as ledger rows,
   but their bodies are in **no queryable store at death** — long_memory's
   2,001 entries are external scrapings and short fragments. The pruner
   ("pruned 2 long memories. Summarized and merged.") and/or semantic decay
   (C3.10) ate the run's only real writing.
3. **Long memory is polluted.** Entries include raw CSS/JS from a Twitter page
   saved verbatim as a 2,000-char memory. Intake has no minimum-quality gate.
4. **Speech is one stuck sentence.** 388 utterances, virtually all variants of
   *"something present but hard to name / something pulling for attention. Am I
   off on that?"* — including 4 within 40 seconds.
5. **The user's reply became goals.** Ric's answer "What do you think?" was
   ingested and spawned three `Open question: What do you think?` goal
   candidates in the production funnel.
6. **Candidate spam relocated the monopoly to the generator.** 1,508 candidates
   generated, 224 attempted, 51 completed; **1,270 (84%) of generated candidates
   are "Understand the world more deeply"** — of which **one** completed.
   `generate_intrinsic_goals` is again the #1 conscious action (45/183 tail
   decisions).
