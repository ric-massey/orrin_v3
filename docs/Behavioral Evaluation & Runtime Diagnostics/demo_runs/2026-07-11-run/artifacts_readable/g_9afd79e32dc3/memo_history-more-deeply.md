# Research memo: history more deeply

Wikipedia/Deep history: Deep history is a term for the study of the distant past of the human species. As an intellectual discipline, deep history encourages scholars in anthropology, archaeology, primatology, genetics and linguistics to work together to write a common narrative about the beginnings of humans, and to redress what they see as an imbalance among historians, who mostly concentrate on more recent periods. Deep history forms part of Big History, and looks at the portion of deep time when humans existed, going further back than prehistory, mainly based on archaeology and using a wider range of approaches.
