"Chads" redirects here. For other uses, see Chad (disambiguation) . Chad is a slang term for a type of young man. In the 2000s, it was used in Chicago as a derogatory description for young, upper-class, urban males. [ 1 ] In modern internet slang , the term generally refers to an " alpha " [ 2 ] or simply a sexually successful male. [ 3 ] Chicago slang In the 2000s, the term "Chad" was used in Chicago [ 1 ] as a derogatory way to describe a young, wealthy man from the city's northern suburbs, typically single and in his twenties or early thirties. [ 1 ] Chicago's Chads were covered by a satirical website dedicated to the Lincoln Park Chad Society, a fictional social club based in the city's upscale Lincoln Park neighborhood . [ 4 ] The female equivalent of a Chad is a " Trixie ". [ 5 ] Manosphere, incels, and internet use Further information: Manosphere , Incel , and Internet slang The 2010s meaning of the term has come into use in incel forums and as internet slang to refer to sexually active, supposedly genetically superior men, or " alpha males ". [ 6 ] Within the manosphere and internet culture, Chads are generally viewed as constituting the top decile in terms of genetic fitness . [ 7 ] In online animation drawings in the manosphere, a Chad is often depicted as a muscular blond man with very pronounced masculine features. One such depiction, in the "Virgin vs. Chad" internet meme that originated from the /r9k/ board on 4chan in 2017, contrasts an introverted and insecure "Virgin" who acts normal, compared to a muscular and egotistical "Chad" who acts absurdly and over the top. [ 8 ] Chads are sometimes portrayed as the opposite to "omega" or "beta" males , and as physically attractive. The term Chad is sometimes used interchangeably with "slayer". [ 9 ] Due to their characterization as being genetically gifted and privileged—though sometimes depicted as shallow, air-headed, arrogant, and overtly sexual [ 10 ] —the term Chad is used in both a pejorative and complimentary way on incel forums and on the internet as a whole. [ 11 ] [ 12 ] The female counterpart to a Chad is a "Stacy". [ 13 ] [ 11 ] [ 14 ] [ 15 ] Online memes The term was used in a 2013 viral meme [ 16 ] after appearing on the online messaging board 4chan under the name "Chad Thundercock". [ 17 ] Chad is described as a heterosexual, white male, usually blond-haired, [ 18 ] [ 19 ] who is gainfully employed, athletic, sexually active, and well-endowed . [ 20 ] Gigachad "Gigachad" is a related internet meme dating back to 2016 that uses black-and-white photographs of a muscular male model photographed by Krista Sudmalis, as part of her Sleek'n'Tears art project. [ 21 ] It is used to assert one's opinion as that of "the ultimate alpha who can never be wrong", or with a sense that confidence is more important than accuracy.
